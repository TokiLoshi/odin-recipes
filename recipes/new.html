Pesto Pasta

Description: Pesto Pasta

This delicious pesto pasta is quick and delicious. Serve up to 10 people in minutes. Expect a prep time of roughly 5 minutes, cooking time of 10 minutes.

This recipe contains 225 calories, 7 grams of fat, 32 grams of carbs, and 8 grams of protein.

    Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
